Biography

"
Technology is not just a tool — it's a language. And I'm here to help every young Ghanaian speak it fluently.

Mubarik Yussif is a passionate technologist, educator, and innovator based in Ghana. From an early age, he developed a fascination with computers and how technology could solve real-world problems — leading him to pursue Computer Science.

Alongside his studies, Yussif facilitates at STEAMerChamps Academy, designing sessions that make complex STEM concepts accessible. He brings creativity and energy to every class, turning abstract ideas into exciting discoveries.

As a Technical Coach for the Ghana Science & Technology Explorer Prize, he mentors young innovators to build and pitch cutting-edge projects that compete nationally — combining deep technical expertise with infectious enthusiasm.

Yussif's vision: a Ghana where every young person has the tools, knowledge, and confidence to shape the future.
